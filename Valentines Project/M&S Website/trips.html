Colorado Springs, CO

August 2023

Why pick a relaxing first vacation together when instead you can go hike a mountain?

Our first hike together was the grueling 13.5 mile, 7,400ft elevation gain trek to the top of Pikes Peak.

We also visited the Garden of the Gods, old Colorado Springs, Downtown Colorado Springs, and had a plethora of (unfortunately terrible) food.

Seattle, WA

December 2023

For 10 days, we visited Max's family in Seattle. We had a wonderful time (even with the winter rain!) and got to do a lot of fun activities.

Unlike Colorado, we indulged in amazing food. From home meals, Din Tai Fung (2x), and Manao (4x).

We also went to Pikes Peak Market, the Seattle Aquarium, partaked in mushroom foraging, and other fun explorations.

But putting all of those aside, the best activity was definitely playing with Max's family's dogs; Mila & Chloe! (we miss them so much)
