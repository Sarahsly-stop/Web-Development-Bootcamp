The Top Movies According to Sarah

Aka... The only list you need!

La La Land

One of the most beautiful movies of the 21st century. A story of realistic love, lust, and a desire for success. All beautifully captured with a touch of music and excellent cinematography!

Hereditary

A true horror and thriller classic. Not just meant to scare, but ultimately unsettle. Shown through tremendous acting and cinematography to die for (literally..?)

Interstellar

Get blasted away into space by this insane drama / thriller. A story of discovery, love, and loss. All accompanied by an extremely moving score to capture the heart strings from earth and beyond!
